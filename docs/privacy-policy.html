Privacy Policy for Tilemate

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

Tilemate ("we," "our," or "us") is developed and operated by Dadah Peter Duro (NOVUM IMPACTA). This Privacy Policy explains how the Tilemate mobile application ("App") handles information when you use it.

Tilemate is a tile calculation and estimation tool designed to help users calculate tile quantities, costs, grout, labor, wastage, and generate project quotes or PDF documents.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Handling Overview

Tilemate is designed to operate primarily on your device.

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit user data to our own servers.
  • All data you enter into the App remains locally on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

2. Information You May Enter

You may enter and store the following information within the App:

  • Project names and client details, such as name, phone number, email, and address
  • Tile presets, including sizes and prices
  • Room measurements and dimensions
  • Tile quantities and layout details
  • Pricing and cost calculations
  • Grout, labor, and wastage values
  • Notes and quotation details
  • Company branding details, such as business name, phone number, and email
  • Calculation history and saved project records

The App may also generate files based on your actions, including:

  • PDF quotations
  • Backup files, such as JSON files
  • Exported or shared documents

All such data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. How Information Is Used

Information entered into Tilemate is used only to provide app functionality, including:

  • Performing tile and cost calculations
  • Saving and managing project data on your device
  • Generating quotes and PDF documents
  • Allowing backup, restore, export, printing, and sharing features

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device, for example by using local on-device storage.
  • We do not operate any cloud storage or backend server for user data.

Tilemate may access device storage only to:

  • Save and retrieve project data
  • Generate and export PDF files
  • Import or restore backup files

You are responsible for securing your device and any files you export or share. While we aim to support secure data handling, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data may only be shared when you choose to do so, such as when you:

  • Export or share a PDF quote
  • Send a document via email or messaging apps
  • Back up files to external storage or cloud services

Any data shared through third-party apps or services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

6. Third-Party Services

Tilemate may use third-party libraries and platform services that operate entirely on-device to support functionality such as:

  • Local data storage
  • File selection and management
  • PDF generation
  • Printing and sharing

These components do not collect or transmit your data to us.

Tilemate:

  • Does not use advertising services, including AdMob
  • Does not include third-party analytics SDKs

However, platform providers such as the Android operating system or Google Play Store may collect limited technical or diagnostic information under their own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Tilemate is intended for general users and is not specifically directed at children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally on the device, use by children should be supervised by a parent or guardian where appropriate.

8. Your Control and Choices

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • Edit or delete saved data within the App
  • Remove exported files from your device
  • Choose whether to share or back up your data
  • Uninstall the App to remove locally stored data, subject to device settings

Note that exported or shared files may remain in locations you selected.

9. Data Retention

Tilemate does not retain user data externally. All data remains on your device only for as long as you keep it, unless you delete it or uninstall the App.

10. Permissions Usage

Tilemate may request access to device storage or files solely to support core app features, including:

  • Saving project data
  • Generating and exporting PDF documents
  • Importing or restoring backup files

These permissions are used strictly for app functionality and not for data collection or tracking.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.
